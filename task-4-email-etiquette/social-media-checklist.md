**Responsible Social Media use checklist**

Do’s

- [ ] Make sure before posting whether or not your post is appropriate and respectful.  
- [ ] Maintain a professional online presence with your profiles on sites such as LinkedIn.  
- [ ] Protect your privacy by adjusting privacy settings and not providing any personal information that could damage your privacy.  
- [ ] Post helpful and positive things (ie: projects, accomplishments, and learning).  
- [ ] Check to make sure that the information you want to share is accurate before sharing it. You do not want to be responsible for spreading false information.  
      

Don’ts

- [ ] Post offensive, harmful, or controversial material.  
- [ ] Use too much personal data(ie: location, telephone number)  
- [ ] Engage in arguments or other toxic conversations  
- [ ] Steal or repost anything that you did not create without giving full credit  
- [ ] Spend too much time scrolling; use your device wisely for entertainment purposes
