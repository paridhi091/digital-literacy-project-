**Stay safe online Checklist**

- [ ] Use Strong, Unique Passwords  
- [ ]  Set Up 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all important accounts  
- [ ]  Ensure All Applications and Devices are Always Updated  
- [ ]  Only Download Apps From Official Sources  
- [ ]  Be Careful When Using Public Wi-Fi  
- [ ] Never Share Your OTP, PIN, or Passwords  
- [ ] Approve Only Payment Requests From People You Know

If You Are A Victim Of Fraud, Report It.  
**Report Fraud On The National Cyber Crime Portal:**  Cybercrime.gov.in
