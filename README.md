**Project Overview**

This project focuses on Digital Literacy Awareness, aiming to educate students about safe and effective use of digital technologies. It covers key topics such as online safety, digital tools, professional online presence, and responsible internet usage. The goal is to help students become smart, safe, and responsible digital citizens.

**Module Summaries**

**Module 1: Digital Literacy Basics**
Understanding what digital literacy is and why it is important in today’s world.

**Module 2: Useful Digital Tools**
Introduction to tools like Google Docs, GitHub, and LinkedIn for learning and productivity.

**Module 3: Safe Internet Practices**
Learning about strong passwords, phishing, cyber threats, and data protection.

**Module 4: Professional Online Presence**
How to build a good profile on platforms like LinkedIn and maintain a positive digital footprint.

**Module 5: Email Etiquette**
Writing professional emails with proper structure, tone, and formatting
